Key Notes

  • IBC classifies occupancy into groups (A, B, E, etc.).
  • Corridor widths: typically 44" minimum for most occupancies.
  • Doors in egress must swing in direction of travel when serving 50+ occupants.

References

IBC 2021, Chapters 3–10

Key Notes

  • Accessible routes: 36" min clear width, 32" min at door clearances.
  • Turning space: 60" diameter circle or T-shaped space.
  • Clear floor space at fixtures: 30" × 48".

References

2010 ADA Standards, ICC A117.1-2017

Key Notes

  • Bubble diagrams → adjacency matrices → block plans → test fits.
  • Client program must align with occupancy type and code requirements.
  • Consider load factors: Office (B) = 100 gross sf/occupant.

References

NCIDQ CIDQ Content Areas, IBC Ch. 10

Key Notes

  • Outlet spacing: 12' max along walls, within 6' of any point.
  • Dedicated circuits for kitchen appliances, copiers, or servers.
  • Emergency egress lighting must remain on during power outage (battery or generator).

References

NEC 2020, IBC Ch. 10
